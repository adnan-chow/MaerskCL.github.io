Game Rules

Match Format

1 vs 1 or 2 vs 2. First to 29 points wins.

Points System

  • Win: +2 pts
  • Loss: 0 pts
  • Nil Loss (0 points): -5 pts
  • Team points split equally between teammates

Examples: 2v2 win → each player +2 | 2v2 nil loss → each player -5

Rankings

Based on total individual points. No team ranking.
